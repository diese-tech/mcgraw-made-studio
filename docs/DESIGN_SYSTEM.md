# Design System

## Visual Direction

McGraw Made Studio should feel like a warm premium craft studio.

The site should be clean and polished, but softer than a SaaS app.

---

## Colors

Suggested palette:

- Warm off-white: #F7F5F2
- Soft beige: #EAE4DC
- Deep walnut/charcoal: #2B2B2B
- Muted brass/gold: #C6A96B
- Dusty sage optional: #8FA38F

---

## Typography

Suggested direction:

Headers:

- Playfair Display
- Cormorant
- or another elegant serif

Body:

- Inter
- DM Sans
- or another clean readable sans-serif

The type pairing should feel premium but not pretentious.

---

## UI Style

Use:

- Soft rounded cards
- Generous spacing
- Clear CTAs
- Product-first imagery
- Warm shadows
- Muted borders
- Elegant hover states

Avoid:

- Dense dashboard layouts
- Too many gradients
- Overly playful craft clutter
- Harsh black/white contrast
- Neon colors
- Gaming aesthetic

---

## Animation Rules

Use tasteful animation only where it helps conversion.

Allowed:

- Fade in on scroll
- Soft slide transitions
- Hover zoom on product cards
- Gentle carousel/slideshow movement
- Staggered card reveal
- Smooth form step transitions

Avoid:

- Bouncy animations
- Excessive rotation
- Web3-style landing effects
- Animation that slows the user down

---

## Layout Principles

Every page should answer:

- What do you make?
- Can I see proof?
- How do I order?
- Can I buy something now?
- Can I trust you?
- What do I do next?
