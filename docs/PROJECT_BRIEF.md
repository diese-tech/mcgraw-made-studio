# Project Brief — McGraw Made Studio

## Overview

McGraw Made Studio is a custom fabrication and personalized goods studio.

The business creates a wide range of products, including:

- Personalized gifts
- Awards and plaques
- Signs and decor
- Seasonal items
- UV printed items
- 3D printed items
- Ready-made items
- Custom keepsakes
- Bulk/event pieces

The business started under the name McGrawDesignCo. The new working name, McGraw Made Studio, keeps the brand close to the family roots of McGraw Ave in New York while allowing the business to grow beyond one craft category.

---

## Business Context

Most customers currently come through:

- Facebook
- Instagram
- Word of mouth

Customers often contact the business through DMs and pay through Zelle, Cash App, or Square.

The website should professionalize this workflow without overbuilding the business operations system.

---

## Primary Goal

Create a high-converting custom website that:

- Showcases past work visually
- Builds trust quickly
- Converts visitors into custom quote requests
- Supports ready-made item purchases
- Reduces messy DM intake
- Feels premium, warm, and personal
- Can later support Sanity CMS editing

---

## Target Customers

The site should serve multiple customer types:

- Individuals buying personalized gifts
- Wedding/event customers
- Clubs and organizations
- Businesses ordering awards or branded items
- Repeat customers
- Customers browsing ready-made products

---

## Brand Direction

The site should feel:

- Premium first
- Capable and versatile second
- Personal/handmade third

The brand should not feel like:

- A generic trophy shop
- A small Etsy hobby page
- A cold SaaS product
- A chaotic craft catalog

---

## Positioning Statement

McGraw Made Studio creates custom gifts, awards, keepsakes, signs, and fabricated goods made with care for personal moments, events, and businesses.

---

## Primary Customer Flows

### Flow 1 — Custom Project

Visitor
→ Starts a Custom Project
→ Completes structured quote request
→ Receives confirmation
→ Business follows up with quote

### Flow 2 — Ready-Made Item

Visitor
→ Browses Ready-Made Items
→ Clicks Buy Now
→ Completes purchase through Square payment link
→ Business fulfills pickup/shipping

### Flow 3 — Gallery Inspiration

Visitor
→ Browses Gallery
→ Finds past work or repeatable item
→ Requests something similar

---

## Main CTA Hierarchy

Primary:

Start a Custom Project

Secondary:

Shop Ready-Made

Support:

Browse Gallery

---

## Design Inspiration

Use the builder’s existing site style as inspiration:

- SwiftDispatch: clean structure, simple CTAs, polished SaaS-style footer
- ThreeTails Booking: guided customer flow, clear step-based experience
- GodForge: polished UI/system thinking, but avoid dark gaming aesthetic

For this site, translate those patterns into a warm premium craft studio look.
